1) Who We Are

gameo ("we", "us", "our") provides a social gaming platform that helps users discover players, match by game/platform preferences, chat, and receive activity notifications.

If you have privacy questions, requests, or concerns, contact us at: riad.laamari@gmail.com.

2) Scope of This Policy

This policy applies to the gameo mobile app, related backend services, and other official channels that link to this page. It describes how we collect, use, store, share, and protect information.

3) Information We Collect

A. Account and Identity Data

  • Google sign-in details and account identifiers (for authentication).
  • Firebase user ID and authentication-related tokens.
  • Basic profile information such as username and optional profile details.

B. Profile and Gameplay Preference Data

  • Bio, profile photos, selected games, gaming platforms, ranks, and matching preferences.
  • Friends/follow relationships and block/unblock preferences.

C. Communications Data

  • Messages you send/receive, including text and optional media shared in chats.
  • In-app notification metadata related to messaging and matching events.

D. Device and Technical Data

  • Push notification token (FCM token) and app session data.
  • Basic diagnostics, timestamps, and service logs used to keep the platform secure and stable.

E. Location Data

  • Approximate location/region data may be collected when you allow location access, to improve profile context and matching relevance.

4) How We Use Your Information

Create and secure user accounts
Enable matching and social discovery features
Deliver chat and in-app communication services
Send important notifications and activity alerts
Prevent abuse, fraud, spam, and policy violations
Maintain performance, reliability, and security

5) Legal Bases (Global Norms)

Depending on your region, we process data under one or more legal bases:

  • Contractual necessity: to provide core app features you request.
  • Legitimate interests: to improve safety, performance, and service quality.
  • Consent: for specific permissions (e.g., notifications, location, media access).
  • Legal obligations: when we must comply with applicable laws.

6) Permissions and Device Access

  • Notifications: to alert you about chats, matches, and app events.
  • Photos/Media: to upload profile images and shared chat media.
  • Camera (if enabled in-app): to capture images directly.
  • Location: to help with regional profile/match context when granted.

You can manage permissions in your device settings. Denying a permission may limit some app features.

7) Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell personal data. We may share data with:

  • Service providers/processors that support authentication, messaging, and infrastructure.
  • Other users according to your profile visibility and app interactions.
  • Legal authorities where required by law or to protect rights, users, or platform safety.
  • Business transfers in case of merger, acquisition, or asset transfer, subject to safeguards.

Core technical providers used by the app may include Google Firebase services (such as Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Cloud Messaging, and Cloud Storage).

8) Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as needed for service delivery, safety, legal compliance, dispute resolution, and enforcement. Retention periods may differ by data type.

When data is no longer needed, we delete, anonymize, or securely isolate it according to our internal practices.

9) Security Measures

  • Use of authentication controls and access restrictions.
  • Encryption in transit using secure network protocols.
  • Monitoring and safeguards to reduce unauthorized access and abuse.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we continuously work to protect user data.

10) International Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own. Where required, we implement appropriate safeguards under applicable laws.

11) Your Privacy Rights

  • Restriction or objection to certain processing
  • Data portability where applicable
